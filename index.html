Welcome!

I’m Huan Hong Nguyen (Noah), a dedicated researcher with a strong foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical expertise. I am proficient in tools like SPSS, AMOS, SmartPLS, and VOSviewer, and regularly use data analysis platforms like Python and R.

My involvement in various publications, along with my data analysis and teaching experience, demonstrates my commitment to advancing technology management and fostering human development.
